Frequently asked

My Bluetooth cuff isn't found

HomeBP only supports cuffs that implement the standard Bluetooth Blood Pressure Service (GATT 0x1810). Most A&D Medical and Beurer cuffs work. Withings BPM Connect and most Omron mid-range models use private protocols — write to Apple Health from those apps and HomeBP will sync from there (Settings → Sources → Apple Health → Connect).

What is the AHA 7-day protocol?

The American Heart Association 2025 hypertension guideline recommends measuring home BP for 7 consecutive days, twice a day (morning + evening), with 2 readings per session 1 minute apart. Day 1 is excluded by default per ESC 2024 guidance. HomeBP automates this.

What does the home cutoff 135/85 mean?

Office BP cutoff (when a doctor measures you) is 140/90. Home cutoff is 135/85 because home readings tend to be 5 mmHg lower than office readings. The AHA 2025 guideline uses 135/85 for self-measured home BP.

How do I get a refund?

HomeBP does not process refunds — Apple does. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your HomeBP purchase.

I bought Pro on my iPhone — does my Apple Watch / iPad get it free?

Yes, Pro is non-consumable and supports Apple Family Sharing — it works on all your Apple devices and up to 6 family members.
