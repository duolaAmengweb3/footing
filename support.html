Common questions

How does the 7-day trial work?
Footing is free to download. The first 7 days unlock all Pro features (Cards, Jobs, Quote, Watch, Widget). After that, the Calc tab stays free forever, and Pro features unlock with a single $19.99 purchase. No subscription. No card required to start the trial.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Settings → "Restore purchases" inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I sync data across iPhone and iPad?
Yes — enable iCloud in Settings. Your jobs, customers, and quotes sync through your private iCloud account. Footing never sees the data.

How do I report a bug or feature request?
Email above. Include screenshots if relevant.
