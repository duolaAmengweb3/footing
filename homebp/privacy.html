MyDiaryBP — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-10

1. What we collect

Nothing leaves your iPhone.

MyDiaryBP is an offline blood pressure log. The app stores:

  • Your manually-entered or imported BP readings (systolic, diastolic, optional heart rate, time, arm, posture, optional notes / symptoms)
  • Your patient profile (optional name, optional date of birth) — used only on the PDF you generate yourself
  • Your office BP (optional) — used only for white-coat / masked pattern detection
  • Your reminder times and theme preference

We do NOT collect:

  • Email, phone, or any account identifier
  • Device identifiers, IDFA, or analytics
  • Crash logs (we ship without third-party telemetry)

2. Where data is stored

All data is stored locally on your iPhone, in:

  • UserDefaults (settings + reading list)
  • Apple HealthKit (only if you grant write permission, only on this device's HealthKit store)
  • iCloud private database (only if you have purchased the Family IAP and turned on iCloud sync; data stays inside your own Apple ID — MyDiaryBP has no server access to it)

We never transmit your data to any MyDiaryBP-operated server. There is no MyDiaryBP server.

3. Your rights

You can delete every reading and reset the protocol from Settings → Privacy → Delete all data. This wipes:

  • All readings stored in MyDiaryBP
  • The protocol start date
  • Your patient profile and office BP

HealthKit entries you wrote with MyDiaryBP can be removed from Apple's Health app: Health → Browse → Heart → Blood Pressure → "Show All Data".

If you used the Family IAP iCloud sync, signing out of iCloud or resetting your device removes the cloud copy too.

4. Children

MyDiaryBP is for adults (18+) only. The Adults-only confirmation is required when adding a profile. BP thresholds for children, pregnant women, and patients with specific conditions differ from adult home BP guidelines — please consult your doctor in those cases.

5. Compliance

MyDiaryBP is designed under FDA's General Wellness 2026 guidance: it does NOT measure, diagnose, or treat hypertension or any disease.

HIPAA does not apply: MyDiaryBP is not a covered entity and does not transmit data to one.

GDPR Article 9 explicit consent is captured in Onboarding screen 4.
