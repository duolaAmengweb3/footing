MyDiaryBP — Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-05-10

1. What MyDiaryBP is

MyDiaryBP is a self-recorded blood pressure log designed for follow-up after Apple Watch hypertension notifications.

It records readings you measure yourself with a validated, FDA 510(k)-cleared blood pressure cuff. It classifies readings into AHA 2025 home BP categories (Normal / Elevated / Stage 1 range / Stage 2 range / High range) for descriptive purposes only.

2. What MyDiaryBP is NOT

MyDiaryBP is NOT a medical device. It does not:

  • Measure blood pressure (you must use your own validated cuff)
  • Diagnose hypertension or any disease
  • Provide treatment or prescription advice
  • Replace a doctor visit, cardiologist consultation, or formal diagnostic workup

The category labels and the doctor-summary PDF are descriptive logs, not diagnoses. Always consult your doctor.

MyDiaryBP is free to download. The app includes a 7-day free trial of Pro features.

After the trial, Pro features (new readings, PDF generation, Apple Watch app, widgets, Siri) require a one-time $14.99 unlock. The free tier always lets you read your existing data.

Family IAP ($4.99 one-time) unlocks multi-user (up to 4 profiles) and iCloud sync.

All purchases are processed through the App Store. Refunds: contact App Store support directly (MyDiaryBP does not process refunds). No subscriptions, ever.

4. Apple Family Sharing

The Pro IAP is enabled for Apple Family Sharing — a single $14.99 purchase covers all 6 family members in your Apple Family group.

5. Disclaimer of warranties

MyDiaryBP is provided "as is". The numbers shown depend entirely on the cuff you use; if your cuff is mis-sized, mis-positioned, or out of calibration, your readings will be wrong. We make no clinical accuracy claim about MyDiaryBP itself.

Any reading at or above 180/110 mmHg with chest pain, severe headache, or vision changes is a possible medical emergency — contact your doctor or call 911 / 120 immediately. Do not rely on this app's category labels in those situations.
